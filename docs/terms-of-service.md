## 2. Description of Service

CIP provides an unofficial scheduling assistance platform for airline cabin crew, including:

- **Excel Roster Parsing:** Automated analysis of user-uploaded monthly roster Excel files
- **Flight Line Display:** Visual presentation of crew flight lines and legs
- **Bid Management:** Tools to organize, prioritize, and track flight line bids
- **Trade Board:** Peer-to-peer flight leg trade discovery and coordination
- **Legality Engine:** Automated checking of rest and duty period rules
- **AI Assistant (Najm):** Natural language scheduling queries in Arabic and English
- **Auto-Bid Suggestions:** AI-powered bid recommendations based on user preferences
- **Salary Estimation:** Approximate gross pay calculations based on standard rates
